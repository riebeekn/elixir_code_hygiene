    Renders a live component inside a modal.

    The rendered modal receives a :return_to option to properly update the URL when the modal is closed.

    examples

     Examples 

    <.modal return_to={Routes.product_index_path(@socket, :index)}>
  <.live_component
    module={CodeHygieneWeb.ProductLive.FormComponent}
    id={@product.id || :new}
    title={@page_title}
    action={@live_action}
    return_to={Routes.product_index_path(@socket, :index)}
    product: @product
  />
</.modal>
